Key Roles in Corporate

  • Executives: Make strategic decisions and guide company direction
  • Managers: Oversee teams, assign tasks, and ensure project completion
  • Team Members: Execute tasks, contribute to projects, and support team goals
  • HR & Admin: Handle recruitment, policies, and organizational matters
  • Finance & Operations: Manage budgets, resources, and company efficiency

Corporate Culture & Etiquette

Learn the unwritten rules of corporate life

üè¢ Office Etiquette

  • Arrive on time (ideally 5-10 minutes early)
  • Respect others' workspace and privacy
  • Keep noise levels appropriate
  • Use headphones responsibly
  • Maintain cleanliness in shared spaces
  • Respect others' calendar for meetings

üí¨ Communication Ethics

  • Emails: Professional tone, no slang or emojis
  • Be concise and clear in written communication
  • Always proofread before sending
  • Respond to emails within 24 hours
  • Use appropriate channels (email for formal, chat for informal)
  • Never gossip or share sensitive information

üëî Dress Code Guidelines

  • Business Formal: Suit, tie, formal shoes
  • Business Casual: Dress pants, polo shirt, formal shoes
  • Smart Casual: Jeans, formal top, casual shoes
  • Choose based on company culture and client meetings
  • When in doubt, dress more formally
  • Keep accessories minimal and professional

ü§ê Meeting Etiquette

  • Arrive on time (or early)
  • Keep distractions away (silence phone)
  • Take notes to show engagement
  • Speak clearly and concisely
  • Respect the agenda and time limits
  • Follow up on action items promptly

ü§ù Social Interactions

  • Be friendly but maintain professionalism
  • Participate in team events respectfully
  • Avoid oversharing personal information
  • Drink responsibly at office events
  • Treat everyone with equal respect
  • Build genuine workplace relationships

üì± Digital Professionalism

  • Use company-provided tools appropriately
  • Monitor your social media presence
  • Don't post negative content about your company
  • Avoid controversial topics on work devices
  • Keep personal and professional accounts separate
  • Practice cybersecurity awareness
